Information Gathering and Usage

  • When you register for KeyFu I ask for information such as your email address.
  • I uses collected information for the following general purposes: products and services provision, identification and authentication, services improvement, contact, and research.

Cookies

  • A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a web site's computers and stored on your computer's hard drive.
  • Cookies are required to use the KeyFu service.
  • I use cookies to record current session information, but do not use permanent cookies. You are required to re-login to your KeyFu account after a certain period of time has elapsed to protect you against others accidentally accessing your account contents.

Logging

  • I do not log or store search data.
  • I store access logs (minus query strings) for the current day and 3 previous days. I may store summary data for longer, but that does not include any personally identifiable information.

Data Storage

I use third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware, software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run KeyFu. Although I own the code, databases, and all rights to the KeyFu application, you retain all rights to your data.

Disclosure

I may disclose personally identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the Terms of Service.

Changes

I may periodically update this policy. I will notify you about significant changes in the way I treat personal information by sending a notice to your email address specified in your KeyFu account or by placing a prominent notice on the site.

Questions

Any questions about this Privacy Policy should be addressed to help@keyfu.com.
